Chance At Life, the Game

by Jullian Androkae

It's easy to think that in a place where things are cheaper, insanely low-wages can be adequate. Challenge this assumption with this game, where you live 30 days in the life of a minimum wage worker in my home country of Madagascar. This might not be completely accurate to their day-to-day lives, but I hope for you to gain a better grasp of such a life, which is far from being a game for those who live it off screen.
